About Us

We are undergraduate students majoring in Information Systems, committed to delivering innovative solutions through our AI applications. This project is a significant part of our major assignment in Enterprise Application Integration.

Our goal is to integrate modern AI technologies into practical applications that can enhance productivity and efficiency. With a diverse team skilled in natural language processing, computer vision, software development, and data analysis, we aim to create impactful AI solutions.

Our vision is to become leaders in the AI industry by providing advanced yet user-friendly solutions that seamlessly integrate with existing systems. We believe in the transformative potential of AI and are dedicated to helping realize that potential through our projects.
